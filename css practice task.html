Arrival and First Impressions

Visiting Japan was a dream come true for me. The moment I arrived, I was amazed by how clean, organized, and technologically advanced everything was. From the efficient train system to the polite and respectful people, Japan instantly gave me a feeling of peace and excitement. The contrast between the modern skyscrapers and traditional temples was truly breathtaking.
